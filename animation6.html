Project 6: Final Animation

Created by: Lauren Kim   |   Date: December 12, 2025   |   CSCI Animation Project

Storyboards & Written Description

Description: A 30-second 3D animation exploring the 12 Principles of Animation using a primitive "Cube Cow" character. The narrative follows a grazing cow that is rudely interrupted by a UFO, resulting in a struggle against gravity and a comical exit.

Animation Principles Depicted

1. Squash and Stretch: Applied to the cow during the beam abduction and the final impact with the ground to convey mass and flexibility.

2. Anticipation: The cow "breathes" before moving, and the UFO dips slightly before shooting upwards, preparing the viewer for the action.

3. Exaggeration: The vibration of the cow inside the beam and the "motion blur" stretch of the UFO were pushed beyond realism to enhance the feeling of force and struggle.
